Sexual Enhancement and Energy Product Notifications
Español (/drugs/medication-health-fraud/productos-adulterados-para-mejorar-el-rendimiento-sexual)
Learn about hidden ingredients in sexual enhancement and energy products so you can
make informed decisions about buying or using them.
Here's What You Need to Know
Many products that claim to help with sexual enhancement or treat sexual
dysfunction—such as male enhancement supplements, erectile dysfunction
treatments, male energy pills and stamina pills—are likely to be contaminated with
dangerous hidden ingredients (/drugs/medication-health-fraud/avoiding-products-
contaminated-hidden-ingredients).  
These contaminated products are a type of medication health fraud
(/drugs/medication-health-fraud/common-questions-about-medication-health-
fraud).
These products are sometimes falsely advertised as dietary supplements, food
and/or all-natural treatments. They often have multiple positive reviews and/or are
being shared through social media. They are sold online and in stores.
These products pose a serious health risk and are not guaranteed to work. They
can lead to severe health issues and hospitalization.

This list covers only a small fraction of the contaminated products on the market. If a product
is not on the list, that does not mean it is safe. Talk to a health care professional before
buying or trying a new product for your health.
